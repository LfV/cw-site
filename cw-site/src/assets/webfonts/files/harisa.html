Ayurveda Therapies

Ayurvedic Warm Oil Massage  Abhyangal

Calms the nerves, lubricates the joints.

Organic Warm Oil onto the Forehead  Shirodhara

A stream of warm oil from a copper pot produces an exquisite state of relaxation, nourish the nervous system and bring overall clarity and peace.

Herbal Powder Massage  Udvartanam

The powder helps to reduce fat patches from the body known as cellulites, improves blood circulation and eases joint pains.

Intense Oil Massage   Pizzichill

A continuous stream of warm herbalized oil massage over the entire body. Help to soothe and revitalize muscles and joints, balancing ones whole physiology.

At-Home Cleanse & Consultation

Initial Consultation, 3 days at-home cleanse, herbs, massage oil and 1/2 hour follow up consultation. The result; cleanse your body and lose weight.

Balancing your Chakras

A massage focused on the seven chakras to promote well-being, energy and blood circulation.

Organic Herbal Body Scrub  Navarakizhi

A warm milk body scrub prepared form sandalwood, herbal rice, and other exotic ingredients. Very effective against rheumatism, and overall pain.

Panchakarma Therapy

A specialized cleansing diet, and several consecutive days of Ayurvedic cleansing treatments such as warm oil massages, herbal steam baths and mild herbal enemas. The result; lose weight and cleanse your body.

Lower Back Warm Oil Therapy   Kativasti

A small receptacle of black gram dough is made on the lower back, around the lumbo-sacral area. Kativasthi relieves chronic and acute backaches, muscle spasms, lumbar spondylosis, and osteoporosis.

Bundle of Herbal powder Massage   Podikizhi

Podikizhi is also known as Churna Pinda Sweda. A type of Ayurvedic massage treatment with bundles of herbal powder. Podikizhi is effective in curing arthritis, paralysis and neuro-muscular diseases. The bundles have to be warmed up periodically by dipping in warm medicated oil, or herbal juices.
